Our Values

Empowerment

Our Story

What are you doing here?!

It was wartime.
And me?
Moving from one resilience center to another, armed with colorful headphones,
good music, and an amazing organization that let me travel across the country
spreading moments of joy and connection in such a tough reality.

That day in Tel Aviv, somewhere between Anna Zak and Nono,
I see tiny, adorable, very familiar faces running toward me.

RIMAAAA!
I hear from every direction!
Before I knew it, my knees were buckling under a sea of hugs.

Behind them I spot Maria from the Ben-Yehuda cluster, where two years earlier I had led theater workshops.
"What are you doing here?!" she asked.
And then, like in every good movie,
we ran toward each other for a big hug. And then came a big invitation.

Come to us

And that, friends, is how Sipurima Theater was born.
By word of mouth, from kindergarten to kindergarten, from hall to hall.
All the knowledge I gathered over the years became this sweet brand where we get to build whole worlds,
laugh, get excited, go deeper, and connect.
That's our gift, and we're so happy to share it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the show?

Between 45 and 50 minutes.

What ages is it suitable for?

Ages 4-7.

How many children can participate?

The show works for groups of 10 to 200 children.
We adapt the structure to fit the group size.

What equipment do we need to prepare?

Seating for the children, an open space for the performance, and a power outlet.
We take care of everything else :)

Can we book a show outside the recommended age range?

Yes! We have content for ages 3 and 8 too,
but these are different programs, so it's better to talk to us first.

Where can the shows take place?

Kindergartens, community centers, auditoriums, libraries, living rooms...
Anywhere the kids feel comfortable.

What about payment?

After a first conversation we'll send you a clear quote.

Where in Israel do you perform?

Sipurima travels all over the country!
Talk to us, we're nice ;)

Are the shows available in English?

Yes! Our shows are available in both Hebrew and English.
Just let us know your preference when booking.
