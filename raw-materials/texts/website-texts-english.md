In Sipurima shows, the children step into the story and become the characters!

and ends with a shared bow. Everyone is part of it. Everyone.

I arrive with the equipment, costumes, and the story.
Kindergarten, community center, auditorium?
It all works.

## Our Story

What are you doing here?!

It was wartime.
And me?
Moving from one resilience center to another, armed with colorful headphones,
good music, and an amazing organization that let me travel across the country
spreading moments of joy and connection in such a tough reality.

That day in Tel Aviv, somewhere between Anna Zak and Nono,
I see tiny, adorable, very familiar faces running toward me.

RIMAAAA!
I hear from every direction!
Before I knew it, my knees were buckling under a sea of hugs.

Behind them I spot Maria from the Ben-Yehuda cluster, where two years earlier I had led theater workshops.
"What are you doing here?!" she asked.
And then, like in every good movie,
we ran toward each other for a big hug. And then came a big invitation.

Come to us

And that, friends, is how Sipurima Theater was born.
By word of mouth, from kindergarten to kindergarten, from hall to hall.
All the knowledge I gathered over the years became this sweet brand where we get to build whole worlds,
laugh, get excited, go deeper, and connect.
That's our gift, and we're so happy to share it.

## FAQ

How long is the show? Between 45 and 50 minutes.
What ages is it suitable for? Ages 4-7.
How many children can participate? 10 to 200, we adapt the structure to fit.

Where can the shows take place? Kindergartens, community centers, auditoriums, libraries, living rooms...
What about payment? After a first conversation we'll send you a clear quote.
Where in Israel do you perform? All over the country!
Are the shows available in English? Yes! Both Hebrew and English.
